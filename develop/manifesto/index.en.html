Manifesto

The manifesto of project AIKernel

  1. The project purpose - creation of operating time for use in projects with AI elements.
  2. Results of work of project AIKernel always should extend under free license AIKernel.
  3. The structure of project AIKernel should include only free subprojects under license AIKernel.
  4. In license agreement AIKernel possibility of use of operating time of project AIKernel in any other projects, as not commercial, and commercial should be provided.
  5. The operating committee with the permission of the president can make changes in the manifesto and license AIKernel. For this change in the manifesto and the license text should be unanimously accepted all committee-men and the president.
